About Arise Medical & Wellness

At Arise Medical & Wellness, our mission is clear:
To deliver affordable, accessible, and patient-centered care that empowers you to live well—mind, body, and soul.

Rooted in a deep commitment to healing, renewal, and purpose, Arise draws inspiration from the Hebrew word "shaked," meaning "to watch." Much like the almond tree, whose blossoms are the first to appear at the end of winter, we believe in the power of new beginnings, vigilance, and the unwavering spirit of hope and diligence.

Founded by a devout Christian and compassionate physician, Arise was born from a desire to serve others with integrity, excellence, and heart. Our approach integrates the best of modern medicine with holistic wellness, nurturing every dimension of your health—physical, emotional, and spiritual.

We invite you to begin your journey with Arise, where you're not just a patient—you're a person, deeply cared for and watched over. Together, we'll help you rise into your best, healthiest self.

About Dr. Wilmot-Desouza

Meet Dr. Nana Wilmot-DeSouza

Triple Board-Certified in Family, Lifestyle & Obesity Medicine

Dr. Nana Wilmot-DeSouza is more than a physician—she's a healer, teacher, and advocate for whole-person care. As a triple board-certified physician in Family Medicine, Lifestyle Medicine, and Obesity Medicine, Dr. Wilmot-DeSouza empowers her patients to reclaim their health and live with purpose.

A strong supporter of the direct primary care (DPC) model, she is passionate about delivering affordable, accessible, and relationship-based care that treats the whole person—mind, body, and soul.

Dr. Wilmot-DeSouza completed her medical residency at the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences, bringing both clinical expertise and rich life experience to every patient interaction. Her care philosophy centers on compassion, education, and empowerment—building genuine connections that go beyond symptoms to support lasting transformation.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Wilmot-DeSouza finds joy in family life, cooking, travel, and faith-based community service. She plays an active role in her local church and volunteers on medical mission trips, serving under-resourced communities around the world. She is also a leader in a global nonprofit dedicated to extending care and compassion across borders.

Experience the difference with Dr. Wilmot-DeSouza—where medical excellence meets personal connection and holistic healing.
